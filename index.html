The Intelligent Systems Laboratory

About Us

Welcome to the Intelligent Systems Laboratory, where we design, build, and understand intelligent systems. We are located in the Engineering and Computational Sciences Building as part of the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Old Dominion University.

Research Areas

Swarm Intelligence

  • Decemtralized POMDPs
  • Emergent behavior through energy minimization
  • Swarming to avoid predators and adversaries

Artificial Intelligence

  • Fast Optimal Control
  • Intelligent Transportation Networks
  • Multi-Agent Navigation
  • Flexible Manufacturing
  • Digital Twins

Embodied Intelligence

  • Optimizing micron-scale robot morphology
  • Bio-Inspired design for robotics
